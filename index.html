Project Info

Goals and Objectives

Our application aims to improve the tourism industry by giving travelers a new view of the places they are visiting. We use geolocation to show travelers places around them that they may not have discovered through a search engine or travel site. We hope that our technology improves the experience of being in a new place, but also contributes to local economies.

Development

Constraints and Issues

The schedule for this project allowed two months to design, build, and test the application, as well as produce functional and design documentation. The team was mainly constrained by a short timeline and not having a budget. Because our application uses the Google Places API to get attractions, the places shown are constrained to the places included in this API. Rural places and places with few attractions will not have as many places included, if any.

Design

Application

Our solution is a web app that uses HTML5, CSS3, JS, Bootstrap framework, the Google Maps API, and the Google Places API.
